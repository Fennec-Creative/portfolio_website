AcuSites

Web Design and Development

Client: AcuSites

Role: Web Design, Front-end Development, Logo Design, Content Creation, Support

Site Address: www.acusites.com

Responsive Modern Designs

The purpose of AcuSites is to create designer websites that are modern and able to stand the test of time. Every aspect of the project was therefore crafted to look good on all devices, regardless of screen-size.

Characterized Logo to Enhance Branding

“Pointsy” the AcuSites mascot was created to enhance branding and user experience. While maintaining a simple design that encompasses the Acupuncture field, Pointsy has human characteristics that are easily manipulated to form multiple gestures and expressions.

20 Pre-built Templates

With the launch of the website, 20 pre-built templates were created with original artwork, icons, and imagery. Four interactive sample pages were also made available to showcase available layouts and animations.

Detailed Tutorial Section

18 detailed tutorials were written and included with the launch of the website to walk customers through the basics of updating and maintaining their website. Each tutorial includes step-by-step instructions with useful images and references.
